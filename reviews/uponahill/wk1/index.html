Ever wonder what's up on that hill?

Apple Blossom is an 18-acre private estate in New Buffalo — gardens, open lawns, and a view that goes on for miles. It's usually reserved for private stays. This September, for three nights only, the gates open for Up On A Hill.

Picture a Wednesday evening: the light going golden, a drink in hand, live music drifting across the lawn. That's the setting — September 2, 16, and 30.

The venue itself becomes part of the show.

Purpose

A night out that gives back. 💛

Up On A Hill isn't just a concert series — every ticket supports Southwest Michigan nonprofits doing real work for our neighbors: Neighbor by Neighbor, Dune Coast Baseball, and the New Buffalo Community Education Foundation.

Three evenings of music, local causes, one shared mission. Music. Place. Purpose. — that's the whole idea.

September 2 · 16 · 30 at Apple Blossom, New Buffalo. Come for the music, stay for what it stands for.
