Meet Neighbor by Neighbor. Real Help. Right Now.

When a family in Harbor Country hits a hard stretch, a lost job, a medical bill, a month where the numbers just do not add up, Neighbor by Neighbor is who shows up. Led by executive director Bob Murphy, they turn local generosity into direct help for local people, quickly and without the red tape.

They are also the team behind Up On A Hill. Every ticket you buy helps them keep saying yes to a neighbor who needs it.

Music. Place. Purpose. Sept 2, 16 and 30 at Apple Blossom Resort in New Buffalo. Tickets at uponahill.org.

Why does a musician featured everywhere from Lollapalooza to public radio spend his year curating a first-year benefit in Southwest Michigan? We asked curator Erik Hall.

Erik is an acclaimed Michigan musician and composer, with festival credits including Lollapalooza and the Pitchfork Music Festival and a body of work praised by Bandcamp Daily and WNYC New Sounds. He did not have to do this. He chose to, because he believes the right music in the right place can give back to the community that shaped him.

That is the why behind Up On A Hill. Music. Place. Purpose.

Sept 2, 16 and 30 at Apple Blossom Resort in New Buffalo. Tickets at uponahill.org.
